## What is Harmonica?

Harmonica is a conversational survey platform that helps groups coordinate, reduce friction, and move forward together. Whether you're facilitating a team discussion, gathering feedback, or running a collaborative session, Harmonica makes it easy to engage participants and synthesize qualitative insights.

## Key features

- **Conversational surveys**: Engage participants through guided conversations that gather qualitative insights
- **Session design**: Co-design your conversational guide with AI, then refine it to match your needs
- **Pre-session forms**: Configure custom questions participants answer before chatting
- **Project organization**: Organize sessions into Projects to track progress over time
- **Real-time collaboration**: Multiple participants can join and contribute simultaneously
- **Synthesis and summaries**: Automatically generate summaries and insights from session conversations
- **Flexible templates**: Use templates to quickly start common types of sessions
